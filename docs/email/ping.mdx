---
title: Ping Email Provider
subtitle: How to leverage MagicBell's smart functionality- but still have complete control over emails. The best of both worlds!
---

If you prefer to set up email in your codebase rather than using an email provider like SendGrid or Postmark, 'Ping' is probably for you!

'Ping' allows you to receive a webhook when you should send an email based on [MagicBell's smart functionality](https://magicbell.com/docs/smart-notifications). You're getting the benefits of MagicBell, yet still have complete control over every email. [Watch a demo to learn more!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I7Ej9oBUhCY)
