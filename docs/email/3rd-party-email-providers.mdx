---
title: 3rd Party Email Providers
---

MagicBell can deliver email notifications for your users via 3rd party email providers like [SendGrid](https://magicbell.com/docs/email/sendgrid), [Postmark](https://magicbell.com/docs/email/postmark) and [Mailgun](https://magicbell.com/docs/email/mailgun). Please [send us a note](mailto:hello@magicbell.com) to have it set up for your account.
